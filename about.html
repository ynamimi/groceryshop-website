ABOUT US


The Happy Fresh Grocer story is one of healthy evolution and commitment to quality.

Our roots go way back to the 1950s, with a small neighbourhood grocery store in Gombak, Kuala Lumpur. Forty years on, Pasaraya
OTK was launched. This supermarket quickly became the go-to place for fresh produce, and two additional branches were opened
in Sentul (2000) and Puchong (2003). In 2004, Happy Fresh Grocer was born in Bangsar Village with its premium selection of produce
continues to serve the residents of Bangsar and Kuala Lumpur with its large array of fresh produce finest goods.Today, Happy Fresh Grocer is a
full-fledged chain of premium grocers across Penang, Klang Valley and Johor Bahru and aims to be the leading premium supermarket chain
in Malaysia. However, we will always be rooted in our mission to be your neighbourhood grocer with a passion to delight.
